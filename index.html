Choosing Your Perfect Finish

A helpful guide to our premium architectural finishes and tailored spreading performance tiers.

Emulsion Series

Formulated for smooth, ultra-durable protection on interior walls and residential block surfaces.

Standard Quality

High-opacity base coverage optimised for clean color tones and quick decorative building refreshes.

Premium Quality

Extended spreading efficiency engineered to glide smoothly over extensive wall areas with increased color richness.

Extra Quality

The ultimate architectural shielding layer — maximum protective wall coverage and surface density for premium builds.

Satin Series

Engineered with flexible polymers to deliver a rich, soft-sheen finish that holds up perfectly to cleaning.

Premium Quality

Reflective, protective glaze designed for luxury indoor living lounges, corridors, and hospitality interiors.

Extra Quality

Maximum wear defense featuring a diamond-grade light-reflecting sheen — fully scrub-resistant for high-traffic zones.

Matt Emulsion & Matt Satin

Velvety zero-sheen finishes for spaces demanding deep, light-absorbing tonal richness without reflective surfaces.

Texcote & Specialty

High-aggregate, weather-proof barriers engineered for heavy-duty structural property defence.

Standard, Premium, and Extra textured block armour guarding external masonry from rainfall and UV degradation.

Gravitex & Trowel Finish

Aggregated marble-chip variants forming an incredibly rugged, stylised geometric facade layer for statement exteriors.

Flexcoat & Gloss Premium

High-elasticity liquid seals and deep enamels built for gates, metal framing, security doors, and industrial surfaces.
